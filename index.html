About me

My name is Badis Merabet. I’m a thirty one years old software engineer based in Jijel, Algeria. I specialize in Web UI, Backend & Systems Engineering. I have more than 5 years of experience in software development and worked with different clients from Algeria, Europe and USA.

For me, the work of software development is both science and art. On the one hand, it grows from the lessons of experience I have learned trying to create systems and programs that improve the stability and performance of every part in product. On the other hand, it stems from the passion for user experience, ease of use, fast response time, seamless interaction that leads users to enjoy using software,and have what they need. I also come to realise the value of these principles:

  • Incremental development
  • Anticipation of change
  • Consistency in coding style
  • Modularity

As a fulltime freelancer, I do understand the importance and real value of my client's time, as well as mine. That’s why I am able to assure him that all of my services will be provided on time. Nevertheless, the outcome always has high quality.
