About me

My name is Badis MERABET. I'm a Full Stack Engineer from Algeria. I specialize in building Web UIs, APIs, and Cloud-Native Applications. I have delivered great results in a wide range of industries working for clients in Algeria, Europe, and the USA.

For me, the work of software development is science, art, engineering, fire fighting, and archeology, all with the end goal of creating stable and performant software products with great user experience, ease of use, fast response time, and seamless interaction.

I also come to realize the value of these principles:

  • Incremental development
  • Anticipation of change
  • Consistency in coding style
  • Modularity

As a remote developer, I do understand the importance and real value of my client's time, as well as mine. That's why I make sure all of my services are provided on time. Nevertheless, the outcome always has high quality.
